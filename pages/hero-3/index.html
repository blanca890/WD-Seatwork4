Invoker
Invoker, known as Carl, is a prodigious mage from Dota 2 who mastered a vast array of spells at a young age, surpassing all his peers at the magical academy of Eeb. Driven by his insatiable quest for power and knowledge, he continues to manipulate the elements of Quas, Wex, and Exort, seeking to reclaim his lost youth and former glory.
Anti Mage
Antimage, known as the Anti-Mage, is a determined warrior from Dota 2. After witnessing her monastery home destroyed by magic, she trained with surviving monks to dismantle magical energies. Now, she roams the world, seeking to eradicate magic and its practitioners.
Pudge
Pudge, known as the Butcher, is a fearsome hero in Dota 2. A grotesque and sadistic creature, he roams the battlefield, using his Meat Hook to reel in enemies and dismember them with his cleaver. His insatiable hunger for flesh drives him to hunt relentlessly, feasting on the remains of his victims to grow stronger.
Shadow Fiend
Shadow Fiend, also known as Nevermore, is a demonic entity from Dota 2 who harvests the souls of the living and the dead. Drawn to the essence of fear and darkness, he uses his stolen souls to amplify his power and unleash devastating attacks. Shadow Fiend's presence on the battlefield is marked by his eerie aura and his ability to manipulate the shadows to instill terror in his enemies.
Meepo
Meepo, known as the Geomancer, is a cunning and resourceful creature from Dota 2. Hailing from the Riftshadow Ruins, he has the unique ability to create clones of himself, overwhelming his enemies with sheer numbers. A natural burrower and digger, Meepo uses his geomantic powers to trap and ensnare foes, making him a tricky and formidable opponent on the battlefield.
